I love painting. After studying Drawing and Painting at Dundee I now live in Perthshire working on custom paint projects to brighten up your day. From smaller more detailed designs to larger murals I use rattle cans, brush, pens, airbrush and enamel make bright individual pieces. If you have a spray paint, sign or mural project don’t hesitate to contact. Smaller pieces may be able to post/deliver.
